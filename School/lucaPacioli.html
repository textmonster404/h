Luca Bartolomeo de Pacioli

Character Traits:

  • Inventive
  • Religious
  • Methodical
  • Unusual

Facts:

  • Was a Friar, Mathematician, and Writer
  • Wrote an unpublished treatice on chess
  • Collaborated with Leonardo da Vinci
  • Woodcut of the porportions of the human face

Life:

  1. Born in 1447, at Sansepolcro, Republic of Florence
  2. Lived with the Befolci family
  3. Recieved an abbaco education
  4. Moved to Venice
  5. Wrote the books, Summa de Arithmetica, and De Divina Proportione
  6. Invented Double-Entry Bookkeeping
  7. Fled Milan when Louis XII of France siezed the city and drove out his patron
  8. Died in 1517, at Sansepolcro, Republic of Florence

Legacy:

Advanced Math and Revolutionized Accounting
